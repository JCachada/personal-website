Please say hello if you:

  • Want to say hi.
  • Want to chat about something.
  • Want to comment on my website or flag a bug.
  • Want to share something with me that you think might interest me.

Please do not reach out to me if:

  • You want to sell something to me.
  • You want to use me to sell something to my readers.
  • You want to try and convince me to let you write a sponsored post.
  • You want to yell at me, argue in bad faith, or generally be a dickwad.

The best way to reach me is through e-mail. If you really want to, I am also on Mastodon.

You can send me an email at jmcs. cachada (without the whitespace) at the protonmail.com servers. If this looks like a strange way to give you my e-mail address: it’s spelled this way so bots and crawlers can’t easily pick it up and send me tons of spam.
